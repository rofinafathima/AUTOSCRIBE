## ЁЯФз How to Change Language

### Method 1: During Login
1. Open `index.html`
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown:
   - English
   - рд╣рд┐рдиреНрджреА (Hindi)
   - родрооро┐ро┤рпН (Tamil)
3. Login as student

### Method 2: In Student Dashboard
1. Open student dashboard
2. Click **"Change Language"** button
3. Select your language:
   - Click on **English** ЁЯЗ║ЁЯЗ╕
   - Click on **рд╣рд┐рдиреНрджреА** ЁЯЗоЁЯЗ│
   - Click on **родрооро┐ро┤рпН** ЁЯЗоЁЯЗ│
4. Language will be saved and voice commands will switch automatically
